Our website allows impassioned citizens of California to select their state assemblyman, view how they voted on certain environmental bills (if they did at all) and have the ability to publish templates for emailing their representative based on how the representative voted on specific bills. What most people don't understand, is that this is one of the most effective ways to create REAL change, this is evidenced by the fact that the 100 companies in the world create over 70% of Green house gasses.
